Your Questions, Answered

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, our arrangements can last from 5 to 10 days. We include a detailed care card with every order to help you get the most out of your beautiful blooms.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 1 PM in our local delivery zones. Please check our delivery page for more information on specific locations and rates.

Can I request a specific flower?

We love custom orders! While we can't guarantee specific flowers due to seasonal availability, we will do our best to accommodate your requests. Contact us to discuss your vision.

What is your return policy?

Due to the perishable nature of flowers, we do not accept returns. However, if you are not satisfied with your arrangement, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery, and we will be happy to assist you.
